FAQ

  • How long does it take to build ?

    By default Pizza builds a distro every 15 mins but skips a run if another distro is still building. Then it all depends on the build queue and the size of your custom SliTaz distro.

  • How long will you keep my ISO ?

    We can't guarantee hosting it for a long time, but we will keep your product for as long as we can. But with enough time for you to share it with your friends or family.
